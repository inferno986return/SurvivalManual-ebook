Further Reading

This is a bibliography of all the books that are either referenced or recommended in this survival guide:

  1. FM 3-05.70 Survival (2002), US Department of Army, ISBN: N/A – officially available as a PDF, though there are unofficial printed books and e-books.
  2. SAS Survival Handbook: The Definitive Survival Guide (2018), John ‘Lofty’ Wiseman, HarperCollins, ISBN: 978-0007595860 – Available in hardback, paperback, Kindle, Google Play...
  3. How to Stay Alive: The Ultimate Survival Guide for Any Situation (2017), Bear Grylls, Bantam Press, ISBN: 978-0593071076 – Available in hardback, paperback, Kindle, Google Play...
  4. The Martian (2015), Andy Weir, Del Rey, ISBN: 978-1785031137
  5. FM 21-20, Physical Fitness Training (1998), US Department of Army, ISBN: N/A - according to Army PRT this field manual was revised and is now Training Circular (TC) 3-22.20, Army Physical Readiness Training (2010).
  6. Handbook of Emergency Cardiovascular Care For Healthcare Providers (2015), American Heart Association International, ISBN: 978-1616693978 – available as a spiral-bound book, recommended in the Basic Medicine chapter.
  7. American Red Cross First Aid/CPR/AED Participant's Manual (2011), Red Cross Store, ISBN: 978-1584804796 – available as a paperback, recommended in the Basic Medicine chapter.
  8. Guide to Local Production: WHO-recommended Handrub Formulations (2010), World Health Organization.
  9. Hand Hygiene: Why, How & When? (2009), World Health Organization.
